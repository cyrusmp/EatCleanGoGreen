Eat Clean, Go Green

According to the National Library of Medicine, food systems within the agricultural sector were responsible for 21 - 37% of all global greenhouse emissions but "transitioning to plant-based diets has the potential to reduce diet-related land use by 76%, diet-related greenhouse gas emissions by 49%, eutrophication by 49%, and green and blue water use by 21% and 14%, respectively, whilst garnering substantial health co-benefits".
